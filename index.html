About Us

Based in Royal Palm Beach, Florida, My Horse Farm specializes in comprehensive equestrian —from installing lush sod and delivering recycled millings asphalt to providing manure bins, waste removal, high‑quality fill dirt, dumpsters and skilled farm repairs. We’re horse owners ourselves, so we know what horses (and their owners) need for safe, clean and functional spaces. We proudly sponsor local equestrian events and are committed to eco‑friendly practices and exceptional service.
