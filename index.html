Our Services

Sod Installation

Transform your paddocks and arenas with lush, durable sod. We deliver and install high-quality pallets to give your horses the best footing year-round.

Millings Asphalt Delivery

Need to stabilize driveways or arenas? We deliver 20‑yard loads of recycled asphalt millings for a strong, cost‑effective base.

Manure Bin & Waste Removal

Keep your barns clean and odor‑free with our eco‑friendly manure bin rentals and scheduled waste removal services.

Fill Dirt

High-quality fill dirt for leveling, berm construction and new paddock projects. We deliver directly to your property.

Dumpster Rental

Clear out clutter with our 20‑yard dumpsters—perfect for farm cleanups, renovations and large projects.

Repairs & Maintenance

From fences and stalls to paddocks and driveways, our team keeps your farm facilities in top shape.

About Us

Based in Royal Palm Beach, Florida, My Horse Farm is dedicated to providing top‑tier agricultural services for equestrians. We understand what horses love—clean, safe and functional spaces—and we work hard to deliver exactly that. As part of our community, we sponsor local equestrian events and strive to support our neighbors with reliable and eco‑conscious service.
